Time
====
Usually, image cleaning process takes about 10 - 13 seconds. It is quite long comparing to the whole process. The advantages of running it on HTC have emerged as it will greatly enhance the efficiency.

The time of OCR process depends on how much content there is on each page. Some pages are full of words, which may take 30 seconds or more per page. Some pages with less words to read may only take 2 - 4 seconds. The resolution of images may also affect the speed of OCR.

Converting PDF to images usually takes less than 0.002 seconds, so it is too quick to take into account.
